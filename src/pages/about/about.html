About MounTrack

MounTrack is simple Ionic/Cordova app used for tracking your favorite hiking places.

In essence, the place is just a set of named GPS coordinates, but MounTrack allows you to give them a short description, or even to take a photo of a place. Of course, you can edit place later if there is something you don't like about it.

MounTrack was developed for MOSIS (mobile systems and services) homework assignment by Mladen Stojanović and Mihajlo Ilijić.

Apart from that, MounTrack serves as a prototype for PeakTracktion, Android app which is to be developed for the same class.
